What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device by a website when you visit the website. The website stores information about your visit, such as your preferences and browsing history, which can then be used to personalise your experience on the Website.

What Cookies Do We Use?

  • Analytics Cookies: These Cookies collect information about how you use the Website.
  • Functionality Cookies: These Cookies allow the Website to remember your preferences.
  • Targeting Cookies: We do not currently use Targeting Cookies on the Website.
