Web 3.0 & Blockchain Services

Solana: Known for its high throughput and low transaction costs, Solana offers scalable solutions for decentralized finance (DeFi), non-fungible tokens (NFTs), and Web3 applications. Its innovative proof-of-history (PoH) mechanism ensures fast and secure transaction processing.

Ethereum: The leading smart contract platform, Ethereum supports a robust ecosystem for DeFi, NFTs, and decentralized applications (dApps). With the Ethereum 2.0 upgrade, it has transitioned to proof-of-stake (PoS) to improve scalability, security, and sustainability.

Polygon: As a layer-2 scaling solution for Ethereum, Polygon enhances transaction speed and reduces fees while maintaining compatibility with Ethereum’s ecosystem. It supports diverse use cases, including gaming, NFTs, and decentralized exchanges, offering developers seamless integration options.
